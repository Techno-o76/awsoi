Who we are!

The Animal Welfare Society of India is a pioneering organization dedicated to the protection of animal rights and the promotion of humane treatment for all living beings. Established in 1983 and registered under the Societies Registration Act No XXI of 1960 (No. S-19170), we have been at the forefront of advocating for animal welfare through a wide range of initiatives and events. Our efforts bring together a diverse community of children, educationists, healthcare professionals, doctors, and other intellectuals to foster a compassionate society. One of our key initiatives is the Doctors Forum, which focuses on encouraging healthy eating habits to promote overall well-being. We believe that a healthy lifestyle is integral to both personal health and the welfare of animals. In addition to our commitment to animal rights, we are dedicated to finding sustainable solutions to address global hunger. Through education and community engagement, we strive to make a meaningful impact on both human and animal lives. Join us in our mission to create a more compassionate and sustainable world.
